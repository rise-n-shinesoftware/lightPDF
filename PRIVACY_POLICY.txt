1. LOCAL PROCESSING GUARANTEE
RisenShine Document Viewer is an "Offline-First" application. We guarantee that your documents, files, and their contents are NEVER uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers. All rendering and extraction (e.g., PDF, CBZ parsing) occurs entirely on your local machine.

2. DATA WE COLLECT
We collect minimal telemetry to improve the software:
- Crash reports (optional).
- License validation requests (Email, First Name, Last Name, and IP Address for fraud prevention).

3. ADVERTISEMENTS (FREE TIER ONLY)
If you use the Free Tier, the Software displays advertisements provided by third-party networks (e.g., Google AdSense). These networks may collect your IP address and basic device information to serve relevant ads. Activating a paid license entirely disables this module and its associated data collection.

4. DATA STORAGE
Reading history, favorites, and settings are saved locally in your OS AppData folder. You can clear this data at any time via the Application Settings menu.

5. CHANGES TO POLICY
We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the Software implies acceptance.
